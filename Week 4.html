Week 4 | Laser cutting

In this week, we did the Laser Cutting to make our own clock. Firstly, My design concept was Avengers. So I put Thor,Hawkeye and Iron Man on my design, change the picture to hairline, and put the file on usb. After that, I went to Laser printer to print my clock, and set up the printer with Mr.Gerber. Finally I waited infront of the laser printer untill the printing ended(to watch any fire), than I put the battery on my clock and finished.
