Week 8 | Soldering

In this week, we did the soldering to make a circuit. Soldering was a totally new thing for me, that I never heard before. We need to solder Capacitor, transistor, resistor, led, diode, potentiometer, breadboard, multimeter and switch in the circuit. I struggled first time because I had no idea about these things, but I slowly adapted to the procedure. The basic principle is to melt the iron with the heat to fasten the things on circuit.
